## [Manthan Patel | Lead Gen Man](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DXUuhMrjapC/) — Video by leadgenman

**Analysiert:** 13:19

### Inhalt
There are 6 levels of Agentech Engineering and most people are at level 1, maybe level 2 and top engineers are sheepy at level 6. Level 1 is interactive, you open a Cloud Code, type a prompt and maybe say through every single step, your agent has 0 context and you are just holding your send through every single task. Level 2 is Expertise, you drop Cloud.MD file in your repo and give it access to memory tools. Now your agent can actually remember context and it is not starting everything from scratch. Level 3 is Delegation, your main agent is calling sub-agents to do the heavy lifting. Your main agent is just orchestrating multiple sub-agents like Code Review, Deployment and Compliance agent. Level 4 is Human Orchestration, most of us are here, where we are running 4 or 5 agents sessions across different terminal, all at once, but you are the one juggling all of them and it's a lot. Level 5 is System Orchestration, where orchestrator agent running all of these sub-agents for you, each one has its own isolated environment and send back to the finish work so you are barely in the loop anymore. And level 6 is full autonomy, you are not writing a single line of code, you are just taking the GitHub issue and then the agent will pick it up, plan it, build it and open a new PR all on its own. You are literally not involved in this entire process and the gap between level 1 and level 6 will be the difference in how much faster, better and smarter you are using or developing with these agentic coding tools.

- Das 6-Stufen-Framework zeigt: GetKiAgent sollte mindestens Level 3–5 anstreben – Sub-Agents für Ticket-Routing, Eskalation und Antwortgenerierung, orchestriert von einem Haupt-Agent.
