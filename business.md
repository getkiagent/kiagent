# Business

## What GetKiAgent Sells
AI-powered customer support automation for Shopify/WooCommerce shops.
- Setup fee: €2k–€10k
- Monthly retainer for maintenance + optimization
- Core deliverable: chatbot + backend workflows + knowledge base

## Target Niche
- Shopify/DTC ecommerce brands in DACH
- Beauty, skincare, supplements, wellness
- €1M–€30M revenue
- Pain signals: no live chat, email-only support, broken FAQ, manual returns
